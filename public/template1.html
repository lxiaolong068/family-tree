Classic Genealogy Chart Template

Instructions for Use

This Classic Genealogy Chart template is designed to help you document your family history in a traditional, hierarchical format. Follow these steps to create your family tree:

  1. Start with the oldest known ancestor at the top
  2. Work downward through generations
  3. Use consistent formatting for dates (e.g., YYYY-MM-DD or MM/DD/YYYY)
  4. Include full name, birth and death dates, locations, and other relevant information
  5. Use symbols and formatting consistently to represent relationships
