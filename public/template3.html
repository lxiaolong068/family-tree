Instructions for Use

This decorative Illustrated Family Tree template combines artistic elements with practical genealogy. Follow these steps to create your beautiful family tree:

  1. Add photographs in the circular frames provided (or keep the placeholders)
  2. Complete personal information in an elegant, consistent style
  3. Maintain the decorative elements that connect family members
  4. Consider adding personal touches like family crests or mottos
  5. Print on high-quality paper for display or framing
